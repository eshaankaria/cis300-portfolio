About

Marketer, builder,
operator.

I'm Eshaan — 24, based in the Los Angeles area, finishing my bachelor's at ASU Online while running marketing for a startup and shipping side projects on nights and weekends.

Background

I started at the Fashion Institute of Design & Merchandising (FIDM) studying design and merchandising. That training shaped how I think about brand, taste, and product — every decision is a story the customer reads. I left FIDM to focus on tech and finish my degree at ASU Online, where I'm wrapping up a bachelor's in summer 2026.

What I do today

I'm CMO at Lotus, a card game brand. I run the entire marketing function — Shopify and the storefront, SEO, email, paid, analytics, and the influencer pipeline. It's the most demanding work I've done, and it's where most of my hours go.

On the side I build. iOS apps. AI tools. Small experiments that scratch an itch. The thread across all of it is the same: I like making real things and putting them in front of real people.

How I work

Two ideas guide my work. First: design and engineering aren't separate disciplines — the best products are made by people who can move between them. Second: shipping beats planning. I'd rather get a v1 in front of users on Friday than a v2 on paper next month.

Outside of work

I tutor middle schoolers twice a week. I read more business books than I probably should. I'm a sucker for any product with strong taste — Apple, Aesop, Issey Miyake, Rapha. And I'm always poking around new AI tools to see what they unlock.
