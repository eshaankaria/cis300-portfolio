Lotus

Lotus is a modern card game brand. I run marketing top to bottom — Shopify, SEO, paid, email, content, and creator partnerships. I built the analytics stack, set up the influencer pipeline, and write almost every word that ships from the brand. It's the most cross-functional role I've held and the one I learn the most from.

Flock Club

Flock Club is an iOS social planning app I'm building with a co-founder. The pitch: making it easier for friends to actually do things together, without the group-chat black hole. We're currently in pre-launch, working through Pinterest content seeding and event partner integrations.

QuitPouches

A small iOS app to help users quit nicotine pouches, built with a friend. Simple, focused, and shipped — it's live on the App Store. The lesson from this project: a tiny app done well beats a huge app stuck in beta.

Iron Man

Iron Man is a personal AI assistant project I've been building solo — an attempt to make a "Jarvis" that's actually useful day to day. Voice, calendar, email, and a long-term memory layer. Still very much a work-in-progress, but the most ambitious thing on this list.
